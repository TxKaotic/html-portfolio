Stephen's Top 3 Movies of all Time

My top 3 movies

Armageddon

Great movie about an asteroid that requires a special mission to destroy it. NASA finds a group of unsuspecting heroes for the task.

Passengers

Movie about new planet dwellers being sent on a 100 year journey in sleep pods. Things get interesting when one passenger accidentally wakes up.

Hot Rod

Epic movie about a stuntman that tries to raise money to save his step-father.
