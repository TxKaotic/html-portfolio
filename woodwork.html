Our Story

Local Roots.
Lasting Work.

BenchCraft PSL was born from a simple belief — outdoor furniture should be beautiful, strong, and built to withstand Florida weather without sacrificing warmth or style.

Every piece is cut, sanded, assembled, and finished by hand in our Port St. Lucie workshop. We work with you from first sketch to final coat, using sustainably sourced hardwoods that only get better with time.
